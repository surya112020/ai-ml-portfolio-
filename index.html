How LoRA Adapts Weights:

W₀
Frozen Weights 4096 × 4096 No Gradients
B
Matrix B 4096 × 8 Trainable
A
Matrix A 8 × 4096 Trainable

$$\Delta W = B \times A \cdot \frac{\alpha}{r}$$

Instead of updating all weights in $W_0$ (which is huge), we freeze it and only train two smaller matrices, $A$ and $B$, which share a tiny rank $r$. This significantly decreases GPU VRAM usage during training!
